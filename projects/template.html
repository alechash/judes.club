Latitude Notes

A lightweight project journal that helps remote teams track launches, rituals, and the momentum between milestones.

View prototype Read notes
Role Design + Engineering
Timeline 8 weeks
Stack React, Node, Supabase
Status In exploration

Overview

Latitude Notes was designed for distributed teams that need a shared sense of progress without heavyweight tooling. The product pairs a calm writing surface with just-enough structure: daily prompts, launch check-ins, and a highlight reel of momentum.

Challenge

Teams were losing context between meetings. The goal was to build a space that felt intentional, but never overwhelming.

Approach

Start with writing first, layer in rituals second. The UI uses soft gradients, glassy surfaces, and slow motion.

Outcome

Early testers reported higher clarity in weekly planning, with a calmer end-of-week recap workflow.
