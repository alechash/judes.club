Spare Pocket

A universal capture app that lets you store links, notes, files, and ideas in one organized place so nothing important gets lost.

Role Engineering + Marketing
Stack Swift, SwiftUI, SwiftData
Status On TestFlight

Overview

Spare Pocket is a universal collection app for everything you don’t want to lose but don’t know where to put. Links, notes, files, ideas, screenshots, lists — if it matters later, it goes here. No rigid structure required, just fast capture and flexible organization when you need it.

Challenge

Important stuff ends up scattered across notes apps, screenshots, downloads folders, and random tabs. Retrieval becomes a treasure hunt you didn’t sign up for.

Approach

Capture first, organize later. Spare Pocket lets you quickly save anything, then sort it with lightweight categories, tags, and lists that evolve with how you think — not against it.

Outcome

Users stop losing ideas, links, and files in the chaos of daily life, and finally have one reliable place to check when they think, “I know I saved that somewhere.”
