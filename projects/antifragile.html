Antifragile

A career tracking platform for software engineers that documents measurable impact over time, making professional value clear, provable, and portable.

Role Engineering + Marketing
Stack SupaBase, Web Extension, Native macOS
Status In development

Overview

Antifragile is a career security app for software engineers. It tracks your real, measurable impact at work so your value is never invisible. Instead of scrambling to prove yourself when things go sideways, you're always building a living record of wins, growth, and leverage.

Challenge

Engineers do valuable work every day, but most of it is forgotten, undocumented, or buried in Slack threads and Jira tickets â€” which becomes a problem during reviews, layoffs, or job searches.

Approach

Make career value trackable. Antifragile turns projects, fixes, launches, and behind-the-scenes contributions into a structured career ledger that updates continuously, not just when you update your resume.

Outcome

Users walk into performance reviews prepared, navigate layoffs with proof of impact, and apply to new roles with a ready-made story of why they're worth hiring.
