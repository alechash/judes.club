Tracktide

An iOS app for tracking peptide protocols — cycles, doses, vials, injection sites, and schedules, all in one place.

Role Engineering + Design
Stack Swift, SwiftUI, SwiftData
Platform iOS
Status Active on the App Store

Overview

Peptide protocols involve a lot of moving parts. Multiple compounds, different dosing frequencies, injection sites that need rotating, vials that run out at different times, and cycles that need a full history to make sense of. Tracktide is purpose-built for this workflow — a focused iOS app that pulls all of it into one place without the spreadsheet overhead. Log a dose in seconds, know exactly how much is left in your vial, stay on top of reminders, and review your full cycle history whenever you need it. Home and lock screen widgets keep your next dose visible at a glance, without opening the app.

Features

Cycle Tracking

Set up named cycles with start dates, compounds, and notes. Pause, resume, or close out a cycle with a full record intact.

Dose Logging

Log each dose with compound, amount, time, and injection site. Quick-log from the home screen or a widget tap.

History

Browse a complete timeline of doses and cycles. Filter by compound, date range, or site to find exactly what you're looking for.

Reminders

Flexible scheduling for daily, weekly, or custom-interval doses. Notifications fire at the right time and clear once logged.

Stock Management

Track vials by compound and batch. The app watches remaining volume and alerts you before you run short mid-cycle.

Vial Volume Tracking

Enter the starting volume and concentration, then let Tracktide subtract automatically with every logged dose.

Injection Site Tracking

Log the site used for each injection and get rotation suggestions based on your history to avoid overuse.

Home & Lock Screen Widgets

See your next scheduled dose and vial status from the home screen or lock screen without ever opening the app.

Design thinking

The problem

Protocols get tracked across notes apps, screenshots, and memory. When something goes wrong, or you just want to review progress, there's no reliable record to look back at.

The approach

Every screen is designed around a real step in the protocol workflow. Logging is fast by default, with detail available when you want it. The app earns its place by staying out of the way.

The result

A complete, reliable log that takes seconds per session and gives you confidence that nothing was missed, skipped, or forgotten between cycles.
