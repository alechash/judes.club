04About

A little about me

I’m a software engineer based in New York. Most of my work lives at the intersection of clean engineering and considered design — web platforms, iOS apps, and AI-assisted tools that try to stay calm and out of the way.

Through Belayer Development, my software & AI consultancy, I help growing companies plan, build, and ship digital products. On the side I’ve put a few of my own apps on the App Store, and I shoot photography wherever I travel.

This site is where I keep all of it — the writing first, then the work behind it.
