Peptide protocols involve a lot of moving parts — multiple compounds, different dosing frequencies, injection sites that need rotating, vials that run out at different times, and cycles that need a full history to make sense of.

Tracktide is purpose-built for this workflow: a focused iOS app that pulls all of it into one place without the spreadsheet overhead. Log a dose in seconds, know exactly how much is left in your vial, stay on top of reminders, and review your full cycle history whenever you need it. Home and lock screen widgets keep your next dose visible at a glance, without ever opening the app.

It is the kind of small, deliberate app I most enjoy building — narrow in scope, fast in use, and reliable enough that you stop thinking about it.
