Belayer Development is a design-driven software and AI consultancy that helps growing companies turn unclear ideas into shipped products.

The focus is practical: strong product thinking, clean execution, and systems that can actually scale — without the chaos, bloat, or endless handoffs. It's the studio side of my work, where the engineering principles I write about get applied to real client products under real deadlines. Most of what Belayer ships looks unremarkable from the outside, and that's the point: calm, dependable software that does its job and gets out of the way.
