Antifragile is a career-security app for software engineers. It tracks your real, measurable impact at work so your value is never invisible.

Instead of scrambling to prove yourself when things go sideways, you're always building a living record of wins, growth, and leverage. The work surfaces itself — a web extension and native macOS app quietly capture contributions as they happen, with Supabase holding the structured history behind it. The result is a career story that's always current, always specific, and always ready when you need it most.
