Spare Pocket is a universal collection app for everything you don't want to lose but don't know where to put. Links, notes, files, ideas, screenshots, lists — if it matters later, it goes here.

There's no rigid structure to fight with. Capture is instant, and organization is something you reach for only when you actually need it: lightweight categories, tags, and lists that bend to your habits instead of imposing someone else's. It's the antidote to the dozen half-used apps most people use to remember things — one calm, fast place that earns your trust by never losing anything.
