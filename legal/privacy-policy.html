Legal

Privacy Policy

A straightforward promise: your data is yours. We don't see it, store it, or touch it.

We collect no data

Our apps do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers — because we don't have any. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking of any kind.

  • No account required to use any of our apps
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No personal information is ever sent to us
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party SDKs

Your device, your iCloud

Any data you create in our apps — notes, entries, settings, or anything else — lives on your device or in your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud sync enabled. That storage is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

We have no access to your iCloud data. We never will.

No ads, no tracking

We don't use advertising networks, third-party analytics tools, or any service that monitors how you use our apps. Your activity is never observed, profiled, shared, or sold.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data at all, our apps are safe for users of any age. We have no mechanism to collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how our apps handle data, we'll update this page and revise the effective date below. The current version always lives at judes.club/legal/privacy-policy.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out at hi@judes.club.
