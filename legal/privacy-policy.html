Privacy Policy

A straightforward promise: your data is yours. I don’t see it, store it, or touch it.

No data collected

My apps do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server — because there are no servers holding it. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking of any kind.

  • No account required to use any of the apps.
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking.
  • No personal information is ever sent to me.
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party SDKs.

Your device, your iCloud

Any data you create in the apps — notes, entries, settings, or anything else — lives on your device, or in your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud sync enabled. That storage is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

I have no access to your iCloud data. I never will.

No ads, no tracking

I don’t use advertising networks, third-party analytics tools, or any service that monitors how you use the apps. Your activity is never observed, profiled, shared, or sold.

Children’s privacy

Because no data is collected at all, the apps are safe for users of any age. There is no mechanism to collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the way an app handles data ever changes, this page will be updated along with the effective date below. The current version always lives at judes.club/legal/privacy-policy.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out at hi@judes.club.
